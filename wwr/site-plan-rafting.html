Rafting Site Plan

Ezenwoke Uchechukwu

Overview

Purpose

As the owner of a rafting business, the purpose of establishing an online presence is to:

  • Increase visibility and reach potential customers who are searching for outdoor adventure activities.
  • Provide detailed information about the types of rafting trips offered, including difficulty levels, durations, safety measures, and equipment provided.
  • Facilitate easy booking and reservation processes to enhance customer convenience and satisfaction.
  • Build trust and credibility by showcasing testimonials, safety certifications, and unique selling propositions.
  • Attract diverse customer segments such as adventure enthusiasts, families looking for outdoor activities, and corporate groups seeking team-building experiences.

Audience

The target audience for the rafting site includes adventure enthusiasts, families looking for outdoor activities, and corporate team-building groups.

Scenario Questions

Scenario 1: What information would a first-time visitor seek on the website?

A first-time visitor would likely seek information about the types of rafting trips offered, including difficulty levels and duration, as well as safety measures and equipment provided.

Scenario 2: How can the website appeal to families planning a rafting trip?

The website can appeal to families by prominently featuring family-friendly trip options, safety assurances, testimonials from other families, and information about nearby accommodations and amenities suitable for children.

Scenario 3: What features should the website include to attract corporate team-building bookings?

To attract corporate team-building bookings, the website should highlight tailored packages for groups, emphasize teamwork and leadership development aspects of rafting trips, provide testimonials from previous corporate clients, and offer easy booking and customization options for large groups.
